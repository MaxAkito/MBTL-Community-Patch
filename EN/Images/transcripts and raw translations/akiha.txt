遠野秋葉
Tohno Akiha
名門・遠野家を統べる令嬢
The mistress ruling over the prestigious Tohno house.

父の死去に伴い、若くして総耶の名家である遠野の家を継ぐこととなった。
At a young age, Akiha inherited the family head position of the prestigious Tohno house after the death of her father.
志貴を有間から呼び戻したのは彼女で、志貴にとっては1歳年下の義妹にあたる。
She is Shiki's 1 year younger step sister, and the one who called him back from the Arimas.

名門の令嬢にふさわしく、完璧な礼儀作法や立ち振る舞いを見せるだけでなく、
As befitting of the mistress of such a prestigious house, she not only displays impeccable manners and bearing,
刹那的な近代娯楽を排した生活を送るなど、自分を非常に厳しく律している。
but she also keeps herself extremely disciplined, having totally rejected the vain entertainment so emblematic of modern life.
そのせいか、プライドが高く気の強い性格で、近寄りがたい雰囲気の少女である。
Perhaps as a result, she emits an aura that makes her hard to approach, with a prideful and strong-willed personality.

血の繋がらない志貴に恋心を抱いているが、幼少期の事故や、その後離れていたことなどへの
複雑な感情が渦巻いており、優しくしたいとは思いつつも、素直に感情を現せないでいる。
They may not be related by blood, but she is still in love with her brother. Due various factors, like Shiki's accident and being were seperated at youth, 
Akiha has a lot of complicated feelings swirling around inside of her regarding Shiki, unable to properly convey her feelings to him, despite how much she wants to treat him well.

本作の物語では、総耶の街に現れた怪異の存在に気付き、
遠野家の責務を果たすべく、夜の街へと調査に乗り出すことになるのだが……。
In this game's story, she comes to notice the abnormality that has appeared in the city, 
and seeing it as the responsiblity of the Tohno house, sets out at night to commence her investigation...
