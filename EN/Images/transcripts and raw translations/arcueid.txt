アルクェイド・ブリュンスタッド
Arcueid Brunestud
自由奔放な「真祖の姫」
The free and unrestrained Princess of the True Ancestors.

生まれながらの吸血種「真祖」の中でも「王族」に位置する存在。
Arcueid is a True Ancestor, who are bloodsuckers by birth. Among these, she is an existence analogous to royalty.
遠野志貴に殺害された事でエラーを起こし、以後は無邪気で天真爛漫な性質に解放された。
In being murdered by Shiki, she began to malfunction, after which her dispositions were liberated, now innocent and naive.
自由自在、かつ、天真爛漫なお姫様。
The unfettered Princess, bounding with charming naivete

強大な力を持ち、日光を浴びても平気で活動できるなどの特性を有する吸血種なのだが、
彼女は人間社会を脅かすことなく、「使徒」と分類された吸血鬼のみを排除対象としている。
Though she is a bloodsucker who holds extreme power, and even has special abilities like being able to function in sunlight,
Arcueid is not a threat to human society, only concerned with targeting and eliminating the kind of vampires that are classified as "Dead Apostles".
因縁の敵・ロアの転生先を追って、志貴たちが暮らす総耶に現れる。
She appeared in Souya, where Shiki and everyone else lives, due to chasing after the reincarnation of her enemy Roa, with whom she has a special connection.
この街で、志貴と”衝撃的””運命敵”な出会いを果たすことになるのだが、
彼女の物語はその少し前、「もしかしたら、あったかもしれない可能性」が紡がれている。
Arcueid will go through a  "shocking" and "fateful" encounter with Shiki in the city but...
Her story is a little before that, telling of a possibility that could perhaps have happened...
