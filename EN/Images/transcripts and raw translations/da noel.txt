死徒ノエル
Dead Apostle Noel
昼の顔は闇に眠る可憐な少女／夜の貌は[ruby=ニンゲン]獲物[/ruby]を嗤う「薔薇園の蝶」
The Fair Maiden who slumbers in darkness by day / The Rose Garden Butterfly who mocks her human prey by night

ある代行者が死徒になった姿。その容姿は『最も輝いていた頃』の姿に変貌している。人間を超越した死徒としての能力に加え、瞳には「薔薇の魔眼」の異能を宿すこととなった。
The form of a certain Executor having become a Dead Apostle. Her appearance has changed into that of her 'at the most amazing part of her life'. In addition to her superhuman Dead Apostle abilities, her eyes have also come to host the power of the Mystic Eyes of Roses.

対象の精神（魂）を肉体から強制的に引き剥がして魔眼の中に閉じ込め、その精神を終わりの無い悪夢に陥れる「薔薇の魔眼」の異能だけでなく、大量に所持する短槍（鐘）を操り、蝶のように舞うバトルスタイルが持ち味。
Not only does she possess the power of the Mystic Eyes of Roses, which is capable of forcibly tearing out her target's mind (soul) from their body, sealing them within her eyes and thrusting them into an unending nightmare; she can also manipulate a large amount of short spears (bells), and has a peculiar fighting style reminiscent of a dancing butterfly.

吸血鬼となる道を選んでまで、彼女が成し遂げたかった事とは何だったのか。弱さを嘆き、手に入れた強さに狂喜し溺れていくその姿は、退廃と自滅の翳りに満ちている―――—。
What exactly did she want to accomplish by choosing to go down the path of a Dead Apostle?　Having always lamented her own weakness, she is now drowning in mad joy at the power she has acquired, shades of decadence and her own ruinous behavior overflowing from every pore.

主武装・スキル
薔薇の魔眼、短槍、長槍
Primary Weapons and Skills
Mystic Eyes of Roses, short spears, long spear.
