有間都古
Miyako
八極拳士を目指す少女拳士
The Fighter Girl who strives to become a bajiquan master.

志貴が預けられている遠野の親類、有間家の長女。小学5年生。
Miyako is the eldest daughter of the Arimas, the Tohno relatives took care of Shiki. She is in the 5th year of primary school.
もうひとりの志貴の”妹”である。
Shiki's other little sister.
喜怒哀楽の振り幅が大きく、感情が表情に出やすい性格の女の子。
She is a girl who wears her heart on her sleeve, swinging wildly between all kinds of emotions.

優しくて物静かな”兄”である志貴を心の底から慕っているのだが、
本人を前にすると緊張のあまりまともに口もきけず、鳩尾にタックルをして去っていく……
Though she truly adores her kind and calm brother from the bottom of her heart,
as soon as she is face to face with him, she gets so nervous she is unable to speak. She'll then tackle him in the solar plexus and leave...
といった奇行を長年繰り返していたところ、急に志貴が遠野家に呼び戻されてしまい、
ショックに打ちひしがれている。
These kinds of eccentricities continued for years, until suddenly Shiki was taken away from her home, called back to the Tohnos.
そんなある日のこと。”謎の存在”との邂逅により、彼女の運命は大きく代わっていく―――—。
Miyako was struck with immense shock, and so on that day, she encountered an enigmatic being who would greatly change her destiny forever...

自宅にある、今は使われていない道場で、身様見真似で八極拳の鍛錬を積んでいる。
Miyako undergoes training in Bajiquan, mostly through imitation, at the currently unused dojo at her home.
その技量は”まだまだ拳士の卵”の域を出ないものに留まっていたが、
このバトルでは才能が開花した状態となっており、
11歳の少女の身体から繰り出されるとは信じられないほどの破壊力を発揮する。
Her martial arts skills had stagnated at a fledgling level, but through these battles her talents began to bloom,
displaying feats of powers one would think impossible coming from the body of an 11-year old girl.
