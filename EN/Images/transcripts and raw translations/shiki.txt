遠野志貴
Tohno Shiki
”モノの死”を視る異能「直視の魔眼」を持つ高校生
The High Schooler who holds the power of the 'Mystic Eyes of Death Perception' which can perceive the death of all things.

歴史のある名門・遠野家の長男で、親類の有間家に預けられていたが、
父の死去に伴い遠野家に呼び戻され、総耶の街に帰ってきた。
Shiki is the eldest son of the esteemed and prestigious Tohno house. Though he was placed in the custody of the Arima family for a while,
he has now been called back to the Tohno house in consequence of his father's death, and so has to return to the city of Souya.

幼い頃の事故により、「万物の死期・終わりという概念」を、
線や点の形で視覚情報として捉える「直視の魔眼」を持ってしまった。
Due to an accident in his youth, he has come in possession of the Mystic Eyes of Death Perception,
which allow him to visualize the conceptual end of everything in the form of lines and points.
特殊な眼鏡で魔眼の発動を抑え込むことにより、日常生活を送れている。
Thanks to his special pair of glasses, he can suppress the activation of his Mystic Eyes and lead a normal life.

平凡な学生ではあるが、死を視覚化できてしまう影響で、
その死生観は成熟し、また変質している。
Though he's mostly an ordinary student, being able to perceive death
altered and matured his views on mortality.

事故の後遺症と魔眼の負荷により、慢性的な貧血に悩まされている。
バトル中は眼鏡を外し、直視の魔眼の能力が解放される。
Shiki is tormented by chronic anemia, partially because of the after-effects of his accident, and partially as the burden imposed by his Mustic Eyes.
In battle, he takes off his glasses to activate the powers of his Mystic Eyes of Death Perception.

Main Weapons and Skills
